WEATHER PREDICTION MARKETS — QUICK BRIEF
STEP 01 / 04
WHAT IS THIS?
ATMOS is a prediction market simulator built around real weather data. You bet YES or NO on whether a weather condition will hold — like "Will Phoenix stay dry today?"

All markets are high-confidence (80–97% probability), meaning you're always on the likely side. Payouts are modest but consistent — and that's exactly how compound interest works best.
STEP 02 / 04
HOW PAYOUTS WORK
Each market shows a win probability and a payout multiplier. Higher confidence = lower payout (safer bets pay less). Lower confidence = higher payout.
94% WIN PROB→ 1.05× payout
80% WIN PROB→ 1.14× payout
This mirrors real prediction markets like Kalshi. The math is honest — lower risk, lower reward.
STEP 03 / 04
STREAK BONUSES & COMPOUNDING
Win consecutive trades and your streak bonus compounds on top of the base payout:
1 win streak+3% bonus
3 win streak+10% bonus
5+ win streak+25% bonus
One loss resets your streak to zero. Discipline matters. The 10% ★ sizing button uses Kelly Criterion logic — never over-expose your capital.
STEP 04 / 04
THE RULE OF 72
The sidebar shows "Bets to 2×" — powered by the Rule of 72.

Divide 72 by your average % gain per bet. That's how many correct calls you need to double your $1,000 starting capital.
Avg gain 8%/bet72 ÷ 8 = 9 bets
Avg gain 12%/bet72 ÷ 12 = 6 bets
Streaks accelerate this dramatically. Consistency is the edge.
